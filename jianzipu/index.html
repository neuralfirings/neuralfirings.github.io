4. Vertical Characters

Preface with : and split characters with , or each character/letter will be split in its own line. You can use any characters that are in the standalone character set, but the font is optimized to display vertically for: 1-10 (), 0 (), wai (), shang (), xia (), gun (), fu (), dao (), ru ().

ASCII output

56
:shang,5,6		 [[[56]]]

:xia,10,1		 [[[:xia,10,1]]

:0,gun,fu
:san,gun,fu		 [[[]]]

:zhi4,5		 [[[:zhi4,5]]]

:ru,1		 [[[:]]]
